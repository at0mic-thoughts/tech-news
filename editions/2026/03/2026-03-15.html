🏆 Story of the Week

Yann LeCun just raised $1.03 billion for a new AI startup focused on physical-world understanding. This isn't another LLM company — it's a bet against the scaling paradigm that OpenAI, Anthropic, and Meta are doubling down on. LeCun believes the path to AGI isn't through bigger language models but through systems that understand physics, spatial reasoning, and the real world. With backing from some of Silicon Valley's biggest names, this could reshape the entire AI landscape. The question isn't whether this will compete with ChatGPT, but whether it will make ChatGPT irrelevant.
